Enhance Your Gaming With Esports!!

Goldsmith Esports(GESports) is a departmental student organization within Student Involvement that aspires to create an environment where students at George Mason University can connect with others and form a community around esports. GEsports also aims to build student teams and foster player skill to compete in esports competitions at the collegiate level.
